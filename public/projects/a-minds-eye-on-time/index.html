A Mind's Eye on Time

DevelopmentInteraction DesignResearch

One timer does not fit all—what's missing?

The timer itself, actually. 

I created this little buddy to help communicate the passage of time in a more tangible, measurable, and actionable way.  Specifically, to help accommodate those with short-term memory issues. 

I designed and developed a prototype with a workflow involving Figma, Javascript, HTML, and CSS.
