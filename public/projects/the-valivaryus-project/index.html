The Valivaryus Project

Web DesignWeb DevelopmentBrandingResearch

How might we address the stigma against people who live with intellectual and developmental disabilities?

It's a big ask!

The initiative I created as a part of my degree project addresses Down Syndrome in particular due to my sister's personal experiences and my own observations over the course of our lives.

My research and interviews led my initial concept to shift and morph from its original 3 separate pieces into this one initiative.
